Digital Art

Digital art refers to artistic creations produced using digital technology, such as computers, tablets, and software programs. Artists utilize various digital tools, including graphic design software, drawing tablets, and 3D modeling programs, to create visual compositions. Digital art can encompass a wide range of styles, from digital paintings and illustrations to 3D renders and multimedia installations. The medium allows for incredible flexibility and experimentation, enabling artists to manipulate colors, textures, and forms with precision.
